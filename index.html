CREATION OF A SECURITY POLICY AND ENSURING COMPLIANCE WITH ISO/IEC 27001 STANDARDS FOR COMPANY XM.

This report presents a draft Access Control Policy for Company XM, a fintech company connecting local and global financial ecosystems. Company XM is a trusted partner for international payment platforms, as it facilitates secure and efficient transactions for a growing client base of Nigerian and international businesses. The company has already implemented and met the requirements of PCI DSS regulations and is now looking to align with industry standards in its policy-making process and eventually get ISO 27001 certified. The objective of this work is to develop and implement a security policy aligned with ISO 27001 compliance framework to enhance Company XM information security posture and verify that the policy aligns with ISO 27001 requirements towards ensuring readiness for audits.

SECURITY ASSESSMENT REPORT OF CEDAR INC’S NETWORK RESILIENCE REVIEW.

This report documents the results of a controlled penetration test conducted on Cedar Inc.’s corporate network. The primary objective of this engagement was to evaluate the organization’s resilience against realistic cyberattack scenarios and to identify vulnerabilities that could result in data breaches or unauthorized access to critical systems. The assessment specifically examined the potential impact of Address Resolution Protocol (ARP) spoofing attacks, a technique whereby a malicious actor can intercept, monitor, and manipulate network traffic. Successful exploitation of this vulnerability could enable an attacker to: Capture employee authentication credentials during network logins; Gain unauthorized access to confidential internal communications and file transfers; Modify or corrupt data in transit without detection, potentially undermining data integrity. The findings of this engagement underscore significant risks to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Cedar Inc.’s information assets. Immediate remediation measures are recommended to strengthen network security controls and reduce exposure to these threats.
