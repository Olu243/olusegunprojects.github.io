About Olusegun Awolude

A seasoned cybersecurity professional with a strong track record of safeguarding digital assets and enabling secure business operations across diverse sectors who has developed deep expertise in threat detection and response, vulnerability management, governance, risk and compliance (GRC), and security awareness. Known for his strategic thinking and technical proficiency, Olusegun has helped organizations strengthen their security posture through the implementation of industry-standard frameworks, including ISO/IEC 27001, NIST, and CIS Controls. His work has involved conducting risk assessments, leading incident response efforts, managing security audits, and ensuring regulatory compliance. He thrives in dynamic environments and is passionate about staying ahead of emerging threats. Olusegun combines a proactive security mindset with excellent communication and collaboration skills, making him a valuable advisor to both technical teams and executive leadership. His mission is simple: to help organizations build resilient and trustworthy systems that foster innovation and protect what matters most.
